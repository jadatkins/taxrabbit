About TaxRabbit

TaxRabbit is a very simple bookkeeping system for sole traders (self-employed people) in the United Kingdom.  It is intended mainly to take the pain out of self-assessment tax returns, with the additional aim of (optionally) helping you run your business by keeping track of which jobs you have or haven't been paid for (to help you chase up your debtors).
